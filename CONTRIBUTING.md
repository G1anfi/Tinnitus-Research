## üöÄ How to Contribute

### Method 1: GitHub Issues (Easiest - No Technical Skills Required)

1. **Go to our Issues page**
   - Navigate to the Issues tab
   - Click "New Issue"

2. **Use our submission template**
   - Select the "New Resource Submission" template
   - Fill in all required fields
   - Submit the issue

3. **What happens next**
   - Our team will review within 1 week
   - We may ask for clarification
   - Once approved, we'll add it to the website
